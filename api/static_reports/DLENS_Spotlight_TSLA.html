Company Summary

Tesla, Inc. (Ticker: TSLA) is an American electric vehicle (EV) and clean energy company founded by Elon Musk, JB Straubel, Ian Wright, Marc Tarpenning, and Martin Eberhard in 2003. Tesla designs, manufactures, and sells electric cars, battery energy storage systems, and solar energy products. The company is headquartered in Palo Alto, California, and is a key player in the shift toward sustainable transportation and energy solutions.
